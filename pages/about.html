Who We Are

Transforming Facility Management Since 2021

GrandSky Facility & Management Services LLP is a leading provider of integrated facility management solutions dedicated to transforming how organisations operate their buildings and infrastructure.

With 5+ years of industry experience, we have become the trusted partner for 200+ facilities across diverse sectors — from commercial complexes and hospitals to industrial plants and educational institutions.

We combine technical expertise, professional staff, and cutting-edge systems to deliver comprehensive solutions that optimise efficiency, reduce operational costs, and enhance the experience of everyone who uses your facility.

  • Customisable, scalable service packages
  • Eco-friendly, sustainable practices

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2021

Company Founded

GrandSky Facility & Management Services LLP was established in New Delhi with a vision to bring professional, technology-driven facility management to India.

2021–2022

First 50+ Facilities

Rapidly onboarded our first 50 client facilities across Delhi-NCR, establishing ourselves as a reliable partner for security and MEP services.

2022–2023

Solar & Energy Division Launch

Launched our renewable energy division, adding solar power solutions and battery/UPS services to our portfolio — serving both commercial and industrial clients.

2023–2024

Technology Integration

Integrated real-time monitoring, smart access control, and data analytics into our service delivery, enabling proactive facility management for all clients.

2024–2025

200+ Facilities Milestone

Achieved the milestone of managing 200+ facilities across Delhi, NCR, and beyond — cementing our position as a leading integrated facility management partner in northern India.

2025–Present

Scaling & Innovation

Continuing to expand our service footprint, invest in our team, and innovate our technology platform to serve even more clients across India.
