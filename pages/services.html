What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive facility management solutions engineered for operational excellence, safety, and cost efficiency.

Security Guarding

Professional on-site security with trained, licensed guards providing round-the-clock protection for your facility. Our guards are trained in conflict resolution, first aid, and emergency response.

  • 24/7 on-site security personnel
  • Trained and licensed security guards
  • Rapid incident response protocols
  • Visitor management and access control
  • Regular patrolling and reporting

Surveillance & Security Systems

Advanced CCTV, access control, and real-time monitoring solutions providing complete visibility and security for your premises. We design, install, and maintain tailored surveillance systems.

  • HD CCTV installation and monitoring
  • Access control systems (biometric, card)
  • Remote monitoring and alerts
  • Intrusion detection systems
  • Video analytics and reporting

Battery & UPS Solutions

Reliable power backup systems ensuring 99.9% uptime for critical operations. We supply, install, and maintain UPS systems and battery banks tailored to your load requirements.

  • UPS installation and commissioning
  • Battery bank design and supply
  • AMC and preventive maintenance
  • Emergency battery replacement
  • Load testing and health reports

Air Conditioning Maintenance

Professional HVAC installation, servicing, and repair with 365-day availability. We handle split ACs, central air systems, chillers, and VRF systems for all facility types.

  • AC installation and commissioning
  • Preventive and corrective maintenance
  • Chiller and VRF system servicing
  • Gas charging and leak detection
  • Energy efficiency optimisation

MEP Maintenance

Comprehensive Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing systems maintenance ensuring seamless building operations. Our certified engineers handle all MEP disciplines under one roof.

  • Electrical systems maintenance
  • Plumbing and drainage services
  • Fire fighting systems
  • Lifts and escalators
  • Scheduled and emergency repairs

Vendor Management

Strategic vendor coordination and contract management that optimises costs, ensures quality, and simplifies your supply chain. We handle onboarding, performance tracking, and compliance.

  • Vendor sourcing and onboarding
  • Contract negotiation and management
  • Performance monitoring and KPIs
  • Compliance and documentation
  • Cost optimisation strategies

Solar Power Solutions

Clean energy installation and maintenance delivering 40–60% reduced energy costs. From rooftop solar to ground-mounted systems, we design, install, and maintain end-to-end solar solutions.

  • Rooftop and ground-mounted solar
  • On-grid, off-grid, and hybrid systems
  • Net metering and subsidy assistance
  • Solar panel cleaning and AMC
  • Energy generation monitoring
